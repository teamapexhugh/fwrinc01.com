About Us

A Legacy Above the Clouds — Six Decades of Tea Craftsmanship from Meishan to the World

Our story is rooted in the winding 36 Bends Road of Meishan, Chiayi. Every challenging curve leads to our tea plantations, nestled at elevations between 1,000 and 1,300 meters above sea level. It is within this environment — where mist meets mountain and warmth contends with altitude — that the essence of Meishan High Mountain Tea is forged: crisp, pure, and profoundly lingering.

Beyond Traditional Trading — A Tea Heritage Transformed into Your Quality Advantage

As heirs to a sixty-year tea manufacturing legacy, we have evolved the artisanal spirit of our family’s tea factory into a modernized B2B supply solution. Our mission is to eliminate uncertainty and inefficiency in traditional tea trading by maintaining full control over production and offering exclusive OEM technical support — ensuring your brand gains a distinctive product edge across the Southeast Asian market.

Reliable, Secure, and Compliant — Your Strategic Partner in Southeast Asia

Our commitment is to be your most dependable partner in international trade. Through rigorous quality control and streamlined logistics management, we ensure that Taiwan’s finest teas reach Southeast Asia with confidence and ease. Our Halal-certified product line further empowers your brand to seamlessly access the vast Muslim consumer market.

We look forward to building a successful chapter in the Southeast Asian market together.
