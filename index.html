(About Us)
PROFILE

Our company specializes in the wholesale and international trade of high-quality tea, serving chain beverage stores, food and beverage operators, and overseas distributors. We offer imported raw tea materials, bubble tea-specific teas, tea bags for tea brewing machines, pyramid tea bags, and matcha powder. In addition, we provide customized tea blending according to client needs, creating unique flavors and aromas that enhance product competitiveness.

Beyond tea supply, we offer tea bag OEM services (square flat bags and pyramid bags), as well as customized packaging and labeling to meet brand-specific requirements. Leveraging our professional import and export capabilities, we distribute premium tea to Southeast Asian markets, dedicated to promoting high-quality tea culture internationally.
